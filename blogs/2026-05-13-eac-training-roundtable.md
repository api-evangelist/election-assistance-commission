EAC Training Roundtable kmeyers Wednesday, May 13, 2026 Start Time Thursday, May 21, 2026 1:00 PM End Time Thursday, May 21, 2026 3:00 PM The U.S. Election Assistance Commission (EAC) is hosting a virtual public meeting on election official training on Thursday, May 21, 2026. During the meeting, the EAC’s Commissioners and staff will lead discussions with election officials on their experiences with existing election official training programs, opportunities for training engagement, and the EAC’s role in training.
