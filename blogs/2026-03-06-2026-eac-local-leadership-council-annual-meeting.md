<p><em>Please note: The above start and end times for this event reflect&nbsp;Central Time<strong> </strong>(CT).</em></p><p><span>The U.S. Election Assistance Commission (EAC)’s<strong>&nbsp;</strong></span><a href="https://www.eac.gov/about/local-leadership-council#:~:text=The%20Local%20Leadership%20Council%20was,state&amp;apos;s%20local%20election%20official%20association"><span>Local Leadership Council</span></a><span>&nbsp;(LLC) will host an in-person public meeting on April 14-15, 2026, at The Drake Hotel in Chicago, Illinois.&nbsp;During the meeting, members will conduct regular business and discuss EAC updates and upcoming programs.</span></p><p>The LLC consists of 100 members. The EAC appoints two members from each state after soliciting nominations from each state’s election official professional association.</p><p>If you are a member of the public and you are interested in attending, please&nbsp;register below.&nbsp;</p><p><strong>WHEN:&nbsp;</strong><span>Tuesday, April 14, 2026, 9:00 a.m. to 4:30 p.m. CT and Wednesday, April 15, 2026,&nbsp;9:00 a.m. to 12:00 p.m.&nbsp;CT</span></p><p><strong>MEETING LOCATION:&nbsp;</strong>The Drake,&nbsp;140 East Walton Place,&nbsp;Chicago, IL 60611</p><p><a href="https://docs.google.com/forms/d/e/1FAIpQLSf6HdBJTetvcva7w54wrXFiwpMwgZ68DUziaLDWSU4yC3gHwA/viewform?usp=publish-editor" target="_blank"><strong>PUBLIC REGISTRATION LINK</strong></a><strong>:</strong> Please note this meeting will not be livestreamed.&nbsp;<strong>Registration is required to attend.&nbsp;Please register by April 10, 2026.&nbsp;</strong>LLC members should have&nbsp;received&nbsp;instructions on how to attend through the Board's Alternate Designated Federal Officer.</p><p><strong>PUBLIC COMMENTS:</strong> Members of the public may <a href="https://www.regulations.gov/document/EAC-2026-0100-0001" target="_blank"><strong>submit written comments here</strong></a> until <strong>Apr 13, 2026, at 11:59 p.m. ET.</strong></p></div>
