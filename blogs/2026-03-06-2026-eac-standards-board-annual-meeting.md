<p><em>Please note: The above start and end times for this event reflect&nbsp;<strong>Central Time (CT)</strong>.</em></p><p>The U.S. Election Assistance Commission (EAC)’s<strong>&nbsp;</strong><a href="https://www.eac.gov/about_the_eac/standards_board" target="_blank">Standards Board</a>&nbsp;will host an in-person public meeting&nbsp;on April 16– 17, 2026,&nbsp;at&nbsp;The Drake Hotel in Chicago, Illinois.&nbsp;During the meeting, members will conduct regular business and discuss EAC updates and upcoming programs.</p><p>The Standards Board consists of 55 state election officials selected by their respective chief&nbsp;state election official and 55 local election officials selected through a process supervised by the&nbsp;chief state election official.&nbsp;</p><p>If you are a member of the public and you are interested in attending, please&nbsp;register below.</p><p><strong>WHEN:&nbsp;</strong><span>Thursday, April 16, 2026, 9:00 a.m. to 4:30 p.m. CT and Friday, April 17, 2026,&nbsp;9:00 a.m. to 12:00 p.m. CT</span></p><p><strong>MEETING LOCATION:&nbsp;</strong>The Drake,&nbsp;140 East Walton Place,&nbsp;Chicago, IL 60611</p><p><a href="https://docs.google.com/forms/d/e/1FAIpQLSefBdCm4whrsl5LfBym_w1OK9DPXNFBTQ5wYWjqd8da5NEcjw/viewform?usp=publish-editor" target="_blank"><strong>PUBLIC REGISTRATION LINK</strong></a><strong>:</strong> Please note this meeting will not be livestreamed.&nbsp;<strong>Registration is required to attend.&nbsp;Please register by April 10, 2026.&nbsp;</strong>Standards Board members should have&nbsp;received&nbsp;instructions on how to attend through the Board's Alternate Designated Federal Officer.</p><p><strong>PUBLIC COMMENTS:</strong><span> Members of the public may </span><a class="ext" href="https://www.regulations.gov/document/EAC-2026-0101-0001" rel="noreferrer" target="_blank" title="(opens in a new window)"><strong>submit written comments
